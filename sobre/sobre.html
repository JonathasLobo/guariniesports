About Guariní E-sport

Founded on May 11, 2019, Guariní E-sport was born to drive the passion for esports and lead players to excellence. Our name, inspired by Tupi-Guarani, means "that which gives strength", reflecting our determination and competitive spirit.

We compete in titles such as Pokémon Unite, VGC, TCG, and Pokémon GO, always pursuing evolution, strategy, and innovation.

More than just competing, we aim to build a community, share knowledge, and inspire new talent. If you love esports, join us and be part of this journey!
